Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information we collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and booking details (ground, date, time slot).

2. How we use your information

We use your information to create and manage bookings, send booking confirmations, provide customer support, and prevent fraud or misuse of the platform.

3. Payment data handling

Payments are processed by Razorpay, our payment gateway partner. We do not store your card or bank details on our servers. Razorpay handles payment details securely as per their compliance standards.

4. Razorpay as payment gateway

When you choose to pay online, you may be redirected to Razorpay Checkout. Payment confirmation is recorded after server-side verification of the Razorpay payment signature.

5. Data retention

We retain booking and payment references for operational, legal, and audit purposes. You may request deletion of your account data where applicable.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the details on the Contact Us page.
