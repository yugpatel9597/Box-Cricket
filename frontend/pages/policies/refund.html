Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated:

1. Cancellation time limits

You can cancel a booking before the slot start time. Late cancellations may not be eligible for refunds. Ground-specific rules may apply.

2. Refund timeline

Eligible refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method used during checkout.

3. Partial / full refund rules

Refund eligibility may depend on how close the cancellation is to the slot start time and the ground’s policies. Convenience or payment gateway fees (if any) may be non-refundable.

4. Razorpay payments

Online payments are processed via Razorpay. Refunds (when applicable) are initiated through Razorpay and may take additional time to reflect based on your bank.

5. Contact

For refund-related support, contact us through the Contact Us page with your Booking ID.
